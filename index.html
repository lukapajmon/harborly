How it works
01
Owner posts a request

Describes the service needed, adds photos and vessel details. The platform routes it to qualified providers in the area.

02
Providers submit quotes

Certified providers respond with their offer. The owner compares by price, rating and availability — then chooses.

03
Service is completed

All communication, invoicing and payment flows through the platform. The service record is saved permanently to the vessel profile.
